Why Tech?

Why Tech?

After half a year learning code and it's potential through online courses, I have decided to pursue coding seriously as a long-term career path. My educational background was in Science & Technology and I believe I can utilise previously acquired skills into the IT sector, whilst adopting new skills to implement into the industry. With my logical, methodical mindset and my creative side, I believe I can create big things and contribute greatly in this industry. My love of building or creating products for end users in previous employments fuels the drive to succeed in all my previous careers and I long to do the same in the IT industry.
